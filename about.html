why choose us?

Nurtured from the seed of a single idea ‘to empower the traveller with easy & instant travel bookings providing comprehensive choices’, LST_Travel is a pioneer in India’s online travel space. Founded by Shreyansh Nishod in the year 2021, LST_Travel began its journey by serving the US-India travel market with best-value products & services, powered by robust technology and round-the-clock customer support. After successfully consolidating its position as a customer-first brand, known for its reliability and transparency, LST_Travel launched its India operations in 2022.

As low-cost Travelling carriers were introduced in India, the number of Indians opting for online travel solutions also increased rapidly. And LST_Travel answered the call of the hour, by bringing the convenience of booking hotels, and holiday packages in just a few clicks. Over the years, we have partnered with many leading brands from the aviation & hospitality industries, creating fruitful partner relations for business expansion opportunities.
