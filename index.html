About US

Nurtured From The Seed Of A Single Idea ‘To Empower The Traveller With Easy & Instant Travel Bookings Providing Comprehensive Choices’, LST_Travel Is A Pioneer In India’s Online Travel Space. Founded By Shreyansh Nishod In The Year 2021, LST_Travel Began Its Journey By Serving The US-India Travel Market With Best-Value Products & Services, Powered By Robust Technology And Round-The-Clock Customer Support. After Successfully Consolidating Its Position As A Customer-First Brand, Known For Its Reliability And Transparency, LST_Travel Launched Its India Operations In 2022. As Low-Cost Travelling Carriers Were Introduced In India, The Number Of Indians Opting For Online Travel Solutions Also Increased Rapidly. And LST_Travel Answered The Call Of The Hour, By Bringing The Convenience Of Booking Hotels, And Holiday Packages In Just A Few Clicks. Over The Years, We Have Partnered With Many Leading Brands From The Aviation & Hospitality Industries, Creating Fruitful Partner Relations For Business Expansion Opportunities.
